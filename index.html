About Me

I am a dedicated team player with a keen eye for perfection. My commitment to excellence ensures that every task is completed meticulously from start to finish, followed by thorough double-checking to guarantee quality. My exceptional performance is consistently demonstrated through my professional achievements and extensive work experience.

At UST and Intel, I rapidly advanced to a senior position within six months of hiring. I showcased exceptional skills in validating and debugging Intel's next-generation server chips. My collaboration with Intel engineers at UST led to my promotion to Senior Data Center Associate at the Flexential facility, where I continued to excel in validating and debugging server chips. This dedication earned me the prestigious USTAR Recognition Awards in both 2023 and 2024.

At Novus Labs, I was offered a full-time position after just one month of interning. Within six months, I became the Principal Tester and a key member of the QA team supporting the Ring project. I played a crucial role in the public release of several Amazon and Ring products, including the Ring Doorbell, Ring Floodlight Camera, Ring Smartlight 2nd Gen, Amazon Echo 4th Gen, Echo Dot 4th Gen, and Tile Sticker. My contributions were recognized with the 2021 Novus Recognition of Excellence Award.

Additionally, during my capstone project, my team and I developed internal software for OGSystems. Impressed by our work, the company invited us to apply for positions within their organization.
